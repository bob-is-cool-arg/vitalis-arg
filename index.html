Personal Journal - Entry #37
September 12, 1994

The days seem to blur together. The long shifts, the endless piles of paperwork, the constant pressure to perform. But the worst part... is not being able to spend time with Ava. She's 10 now, can you believe it? The time has passed so quickly, and yet, I feel like I've missed so much of it. I keep telling myself it's for her, that it's all for her future. But, I can't help but wonder... does she even know who I am anymore? She's growing up so fast, and I'm barely around to witness it. I wish I could be there more for her, but this job... it keeps pulling me away.

I can't stop thinking about her. I need to make this all worth it. For her.
