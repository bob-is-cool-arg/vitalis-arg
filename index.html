Personal Journal

April 29, 2005. Life within Vitalis Research has always been intense. For years, I’ve worked to build something meaningful, not just for the world, but for Ava. She was born in 1994, and now, at 11 years old, she’s my driving force. Everything I do is to create a better future for her—one where she’s safe and the horrors of this world no longer reach her innocent heart.

I question myself often, wondering if the means I use will ever justify the ends. Vitalis has secrets even I don't fully understand. But for Ava’s sake, I keep going.
