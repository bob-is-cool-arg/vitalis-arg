Author: Bob Williams

I’ve been working tirelessly on the project, balancing my responsibilities as a researcher and as a father. Ava is brilliant but so young. We’re pushing boundaries, but at what cost?

Security is a growing concern. For now, I’ve used her birth year in combination with her name for system passwords. A small but significant reminder of why I’m doing this.

The experiments have shown promise, but we’ve only scratched the surface. I’m worried about what this all means for her future.
