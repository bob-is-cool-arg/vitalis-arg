The purpose of this document is to outline the progress and continuing risks surrounding the research project initiated under Vitalis Research's auspices. We have encountered both unprecedented breakthroughs and significant complications.

The following research concerns the manipulation of human subjects, involving genetic experimentation, psychological stressors, and overall well-being monitoring. The project's nature is classified; however, I can state that we are pushing boundaries on what is humanly possible. The outcome could change the very fabric of human understanding, but not without cost.

The latest report indicates that one subject, identified by their codename "Ava," has demonstrated a remarkable tolerance to the adverse conditions. While the risks are evident, the subjects’ ability to endure these stressors gives us hope for future developments.

As noted in my earlier communications, the involvement of family has become a point of personal conflict. My daughter, Ava Williams, who was unknowingly entangled in this project, will never fully comprehend the extent of my involvement. I’ve kept this hidden from her, but the constant reminder of her name on the security system—“Ava-1994”—haunts me daily.

I must clarify that the future implications of this project far outweigh the personal cost. However, my priority remains her safety, and I must ensure that no harm comes her way. This document is my final warning to anyone who might take my place in this work—do not underestimate what we are creating here.
